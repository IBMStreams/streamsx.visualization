Sharing READ Dashboards

Follow these steps to make your READ app and dashboards publicly accessible.
  • Navigate to the streamsx.visualization/READ.share folder in a terminal. export MONGO_URL="mongodb://localhost:3001/meteor".
  • Find the public facing IP address for your machine (for example, using the ifconfig command if you are working on a mac or linux). Suppose 8.74.12.82 is the public facing IP of the machine. In the same terminal as above, meteor --port 8.74.12.82:3002.
  • Uncheck the private button for your app in the apps tab.
  • In the apps tab for your app, within the field labeled READ.Sharing service URL, enter the URL where the READ.share service is running (http://8.74.12.82:3002 in our example).
  • Use the public link for app generated by READ to share your READ app publicly.
  • While creating URL based datasets within your READ app, use only publicly accessible URLs of hostnames.
