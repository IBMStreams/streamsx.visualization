Dashboard Sharing

  • Make your READ app public (uncheck private in the apps tab).
  • Provide the public URL of your READ.Sharing service. http://localhost:3002 is bad. Lets say 8.74.12.82 is the public IP of the machine where you started the READ.share service. http://8.74.12.82:3002 is good.
  • Use the public link for app generated by READ to share your READ app.
